Built by a Revenue-Driven Staffing Operator

Randy Del-Ray

Founder & CEO, R3 Staffing

Im Randy Del-Ray, founder of R3 Staffing. I work with growth-focused companies that need more than resumes. They need salespeople who produce revenue.

R3 was built on a simple principle: hiring should be structured, performance-based, and engineered for scale. Too many companies rely on guesswork, inflated resumes, or volume recruiting that wastes time and leadership bandwidth. I built a system that removes that noise.

Every placement is vetted through measurable indicators, real sales performance, and proof of execution. No filler candidates. No random interviews. Just qualified revenue talent that can ramp quickly and contribute immediately.

If you are serious about building a sales team that performs under pressure and scales with your growth, R3 was built for you.

Your Questions

Find answers to common questions about our hiring process, candidate vetting, timelines, and partnership model.

Contect Me

What makes R3 different from traditional recruiters?

R3 does not operate as a volume-based recruiting firm. Every candidate is vetted through performance indicators, recorded sales calls, and structured evaluation systems. We prioritize revenue-producing talent over resume stacking.

What types of roles do you recruit for?

We specialize in performance-driven sales roles including SDRs, setters, closers, account executives, and hybrid revenue positions. If the role directly impacts revenue, we can structure a targeted search.

How long does the hiring process take?

Qualified candidates are typically delivered within days, not months. Timeline depends on role complexity and performance requirements, but speed does not compromise quality.

How are candidates vetted?

Every candidate goes through structured screening, performance validation, and proof-based evaluation. We review close rates, show rates, call recordings, and measurable revenue outcomes before presenting talent.

Do you work with specific industries?

We primarily partner with high-ticket, performance-driven environments including coaching, agencies, digital services, and growing sales organizations.

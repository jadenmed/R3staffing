FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

How fast can you place someone?

Most placements happen in 10–14 days depending on role clarity, comp, and interview speed.

What does “proof-first vetting” mean?

We validate performance using scorecards, call reviews/recordings (when available), KPI alignment, and role-fit indicators — not just resumes.

Do you hire for setters and closers?

Yes — setters, closers, SDRs, and sales leadership. We’ll align the seat to your offer, deal size, and sales motion.

What do you need from us to start?

Role, comp range, KPIs, target start date, and a quick overview of your offer + funnel. The clearer the input, the faster the placement.

Is this contract, full-time, or both?

Both. We’ll recommend the best structure based on your ramp, cash flow, and targets.

Do you replace candidates if it doesn’t work?

Typically yes, within agreed terms. Final details depend on role scope and placement type.
